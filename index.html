TechCraft Challenge Registration Form

Welcome to the YantraYodha Club Electronics Project Competition, organized by ABACUS College's premier electronics hardware project club, YantraYodha. This competition is an exciting opportunity for electronics enthusiasts to showcase their innovative projects, demonstrate their skills, and compete for recognition and prizes.

Payment Process:

- Pay the registration fee of Rs. 200 via the provided QR code or upi ID.
- Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation and note down the Transaction ID number.

Form Submission:

- After completing the payment, click on the "Next" button.
- You will be redirected to a form page. On this page, a popup will appear prompting you to enter the transaction ID
- Enter the transaction ID into the popup and submit it.
- You will then be redirected to the TechCraft Challenge Registration Form page.
- Fill in all the required data accurately.

Submission Deadline:

- Ensure all information is entered correctly before submission.
- Submission must be completed within the designated time frame.

Competition Rules:

- Each team can consist of a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 5 members.
- Each team will have 20 minutes to explain and demonstrate their project during the competition.

Prizes:

- 1st Prize: Rs. 1,200
- 2nd Prize: Rs. 600
- 3rd Prize: Rs. 300

Contact Information:

- For any queries or problems during the registration process, please contact our Event Coordinator From our Organizers Page.
