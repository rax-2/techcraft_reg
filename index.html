TechCraft Challenge Registration Form

Welcome to the TechCraft Competition, Organized by ABACUS College's premier hardware project club, YantraYodha (ECE). This competition is an exciting opportunity for electronics enthusiasts to showcase their innovative projects, demonstrate their skills, and compete for recognition and prizes.

Payment Process:

- Pay the registration fee of Rs. 300 via the provided QR code or Click on 'Open UPI Application' Button if your Payment application installed on this device.
- Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation and note down the Transaction ID number.

Form Submission:

- After completing the payment, click on the "Next" button.
- You will be redirected to a form page. On this page, a popup will appear prompting you to enter the transaction ID
- Enter your UPI transaction ID in the popup and submit it.
- Then you will be redirected to the TechCraft Challenge Registration Form page.
- Fill in all the required data accurately.

Submission Deadline:

- Ensure all information is entered correctly before submission.
- Submission must be completed within the 15 minutes.

Competition Rules:

- Each team can consist of a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 5 members.
- Each team will have 20 minutes to explain and demonstrate their project during the competition.

Prizes:

- 1st Prize: Rs. 5,000
- 2nd Prize: Rs. 3,000
- 3rd Prize: Rs. 1,000

Contact Information:

- For any queries or problems during the registration process, please contact our Event Coordinator From our Organizers Page.
